Why we build snacks around seeds

Seeds are naturally nutrient-dense. They bring crunch, flavor, and slow, steady energy—without relying on artificial ingredients or heavy processing.

Meet our core seeds

Each seed in our mixes plays a specific role—from texture and taste to fiber, minerals, and healthy fats.

Chia

Tiny, powerful, filling

Chia seeds are rich in fiber and plant-based omega-3s that support heart health and satiety.

You’ll find them in: breakfast-style mixes and crunchy toppings.

Flax

Gentle nutty flavor

Flax seeds add subtle flavor and beneficial fats, making our crackers more nourishing and satisfying.

You’ll find them in: multiseed crackers and energy blends.

Pumpkin

Mineral-rich crunch

Pumpkin seeds provide magnesium, iron, and deep crunch—ideal for savory mixes and snackable blends.

You’ll find them in: savory mixes and grab-and-go snacks.

Sunflower

Toasty & comforting

Sunflower seeds offer a familiar, toasty taste and a natural source of vitamin E.

You’ll find them in: trail-style mixes and lighter blends.

Sesame

Golden, classic crunch

Sesame seeds bring a traditional flavor profile and delicate crunch to bars and crackers.

You’ll find them in: sesame bars and crunchy toppings.

Simple ways to enjoy more seeds

A few small changes can turn everyday snacks into something more balanced and nourishing.

  • Sprinkle a handful of seeds over yogurt, labneh, or smoothie bowls.
  • Use seed crackers instead of chips with hummus or your favorite dips.
  • Add seeds on top of salads or soups to upgrade texture and healthy fats.
  • Keep a pack in your bag, office, or car for clean on-the-go snacking.
  • Pair a seed bar with coffee or tea for a satisfying mid-morning or afternoon break.

Over time, these small choices add up—supporting better energy, digestion, and overall balance.
