Our Mission

At the heart of our business is a simple belief: that real food made from real ingredients can nourish both body and soul. Our mission is to craft wholesome, delicious crackers using nature’s most powerful ingredients—seeds, grains, and spices—without any artificial additives or preservatives.

We’re committed to promoting wellness through food that’s crunchy, clean, and full of goodness. Whether you're fueling your day or sharing a snack with loved ones, we want every bite to reflect our passion for healthy living, honesty, and flavor.

Meet our Founder

Hi, I'm Nisrine Nader, the heart behind Super Healthy. My journey began with a simple desire to create snacks that were not only delicious but also genuinely good for you. As a nutrition enthusiast and a foodie, I was frustrated by the lack of wholesome options available. This led me to experiment in my kitchen, blending seeds, grains, and spices to craft crackers that are as nutritious as they are tasty.
